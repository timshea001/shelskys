Common Questions

How are the latkes shipped?

Latkes are made fresh, packed in insulated containers with ice packs, and shipped overnight. They arrive ready to heat and eat.

How do I reheat them?

For best results, reheat in a 375F oven for 8-10 minutes until crispy. You can also pan-fry them in a little oil. We do not recommend microwaving.

Can I freeze them?

Yes. Freeze immediately upon arrival and they'll keep for up to 3 months. Reheat directly from frozen in the oven.

Do you ship to all 50 states?

We ship to all 50 states via overnight delivery. Free shipping on orders over $300.
