Common Questions

How does shipping work?
Every package ships free via UPS with insulated packaging and ice packs. Your order is packed fresh in Brooklyn and arrives within 2-3 business days to all 50 states.
Will the fish arrive fresh?
Yes. Everything is packed cold with commercial ice packs in insulated containers. We've shipped thousands of orders nationwide. Your fish will arrive cold and ready to eat.
How much fish should I order?
A half pound of smoked salmon feeds 2-3 people as part of a bagel spread. For a larger group, we recommend one of our brunch packages which include fish, bagels, cream cheese, and more.
Is the fish sliced before shipping?
Yes. Every order is hand-sliced to order in our Brooklyn shop right before packing. You receive it ready to plate and serve.
What's the difference between Nova, lox, and gravlax?
Nova (short for Nova Scotia) is lightly cold-smoked salmon — it's what most people think of as "lox." Belly lox is the old-school original: salt-cured, unsmoked, intensely briny. Gravlax is cured with salt, sugar, and dill — Scandinavian style, no smoke. Each is a different experience.
Do you ship individual items or only packages?
We ship both. Individual fish items ship with calculated shipping based on your location. For the best value, our brunch packages include free shipping and a curated spread.
