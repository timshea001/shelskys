Pro Hosting Tips

Set Up in 10 Minutes

Everything arrives ready to serve. Just unwrap, arrange on platters, and you're done. We recommend a few serving platters and a cutting board.

Serve Fish at Cool Temp

Take fish out of the fridge 15-20 minutes before serving. It tastes best slightly cool, not cold.

Toast the Bagels

A quick toast brings out the best texture‚Äîcrispy outside, chewy inside. Toast just before serving.

Build-Your-Own Bar

Let guests assemble their own bagels. Set out fish, cream cheese, and fixings buffet-style.

Common Questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering 3-5 days before your brunch. For large orders or holiday weekends, order at least a week ahead.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Nope! Everything arrives ready to serve. Just unwrap and arrange. We recommend having a cutting board and a few platters handy.

Can I customize the packages?

Our packages are pre-set for the best experience, but you can always order a la carte items to add on. Contact us for special dietary requests.

What about dietary restrictions?

Our fish is gluten-free. We carry gluten-free bagels from The Great Knead. All products are kosher-style (not certified kosher). Contact us for specific allergen questions.
