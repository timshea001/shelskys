Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How is the caviar shipped?

All caviar ships overnight in temperature-controlled insulated packaging with gel ice packs. We include tracking so you know exactly when to expect delivery.

How long does caviar stay fresh?

Unopened caviar keeps 4-6 weeks refrigerated. Once opened, consume within 3 days for best quality. Store at 28-32°F in the coldest part of your refrigerator.

What's included with my order?

Orders over $200 include complimentary mother-of-pearl serving spoons. All orders include detailed tasting notes, serving suggestions, and storage instructions.
